UX SKILLS

UX Research

Pulling on my background in anthropology, I create surveys, design, carry out and synthesize interviews, and perform usability tests to understand users and their needs.

Empathy

I try to understand users from their own perspective, as well as from an objective perspective. I gauge their technological competency, products and brands they use, and where they casually “hang out” online.

Qualitative Interviews

I use interviews to gather qualitative data about participants’ background, home life, interests, personality, disposition, likes, and pain points. This helps inform user personas and enhance the understanding of users across teams.
